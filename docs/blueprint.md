# **App Name**: KOH Landing

## Core Features:

- Company Overview: Showcase company history, mission, and values through text and imagery.
- Services Offered: List construction services with descriptions and visuals (e.g., residential, commercial, renovation).
- Why Choose Us Section: Highlight unique selling points with concise text and supporting icons.
- Image Gallery: Display a portfolio of past construction projects with high-quality images.
- Contact Information: Display the contact info prominently (phone, email, address) and include a map of the company's location.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1E3A8A), inspired by trustworthiness and competence, for conveying reliability.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#F0F4F8), same hue family as primary for visual coherence, desaturated for softness and neutrality.
- Accent color: Yellow-orange (#EAB308) analogous to the deep blue, providing contrast.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headings and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text. The former will convey a precise contemporary feel, while the later supports readability.
- Use simple, professional icons to represent services and key features. Maintain consistency in style and color.
- Employ a clean, modern layout with clear sections and plenty of whitespace. Ensure the site is fully responsive across devices.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations on scroll to guide the user's eye and create a more engaging experience.
